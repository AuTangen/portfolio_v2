About

former brewer - turned web developer

My interest in coding began many years ago, scripting video game scenarios for my friends and I to play. I became deeply infatuated with the idea of giving life to these lines of seemlingly spiritless syntax - to create something that is able to be felt and experienced.

Life has taken many twists and turns since then, but I've carried this passion to create in everything I do. For me, there is no greater sense of accomplishment than seeing others find joy in the experiences you've curated.

Most recently, this desire to deliver engaging user experiences has led me to the world of Web Development. I've just completed the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp through Rutgers University and am seeking to further expand my skillset and help build accessable, inclusive web applications
